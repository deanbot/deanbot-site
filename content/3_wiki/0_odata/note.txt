## OData filter syntax

`eq`
: Equal to

`ne`
: Not equal to

`gt`
: Greater than

`ge`
: Greater than or equal to

`lt`
: Less than

`le`
: Less than or equal to

`and`
: Logical and

`or`
: Logical or

`not`
: Logical negation

`add`
: Addition

`sub`
: Subtraction

`mul`
: Multiplication

`div` 
: Division

`mod`
: Modulo

`( )`
: Precedence grouping
