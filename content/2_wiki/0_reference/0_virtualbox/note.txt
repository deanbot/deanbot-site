**List vms**
`vboxmanage list vms`
or
`vboxmanage list runningvms`

**Start vm**
`vboxmanage startvm <name or UUID>`

**Pause or Resume**
`vboxmanage controlvm <name or UUID> pause`
`vboxmanage controlvm <name or UUID> resume`

**Poweroff / Shutdown**
`vboxmanage controlvm <name or UUID> poweroff`
